We may collect personal information about you in various ways:

Usage Data and Automatically Collected Information

When you access the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and Browse activity:

  • **Log Data:** Includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring/exit pages, access times, and other usage data.
